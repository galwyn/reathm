Account Deletion

To delete your Reathm account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the Reathm app on your device.
  2. Tap on your profile picture in the top-right corner of the home screen.
  3. Select "Delete Account" from the menu.
  4. Confirm that you want to delete your account in the dialog that appears.

Once you confirm, your account and all of your data, including your affirmations, activities, and history, will be permanently deleted.
