Privacy Policy for Reathm

Effective date: September 7, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Reathm ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Information you provide directly:
    • When you sign up for Reathm, we use Google Sign-In to authenticate you. We collect your name, email address, and profile picture from your Google account.
    • We also collect the activities you want to track, your accomplishments, and your feedback on the affirmations you receive.
  • Information we collect automatically:
    • We use Firebase to store your data and to authenticate you. Firebase may collect some data automatically, as described in their privacy policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Service.
  • Personalize your experience.
  • Generate affirmations and encouragement for you.
  • Communicate with you about your account and our Service.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as described in this Privacy Policy.

  • We may share your information with Google, as they provide the authentication and cloud storage services for our app.
  • We may share your information with Google's Generative AI service to generate affirmations and encouragement for you.
  • We may disclose your information if we are required to do so by law.

4. Your Choices

You can access and update your information through the app. You can also delete your account at any time. When you delete your account, we will delete all of your data from our servers.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information from our servers.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at developer@reathm.com.
